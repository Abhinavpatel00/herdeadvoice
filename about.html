Dedication

This book is dedicated to the girls and women whose voices have been silenced by sexual abuse and oppression.

May this book serve as a testament to your strength, your resilience, and your unwavering spirit.

To the society and the loved ones of those girls: she is suffering, she needs to be heard. Let her screams shout so loud that they tremble the monster's soul for such an act he did. Let her be the voice for herself and her sisters who suffered the same way in this world. Some still live with hearts full of injuries, and some found relief by dying with such intense pain.

We see you, we hear you, and we stand with you in solidarity.

May your stories ignite a flame of hope and spark a revolution of change.

This powerful and moving poetry book is a heartfelt effort to bring change to the world by planting the seeds of kindness, mercy, and compassion in the minds of younger generations. With a focus on the importance of consent and the insufferable impact of abuse, the author hopes to inspire, especially the new generation, to create a better tomorrow, one that is free from the curse of exploitation and domination.

Through heartfelt and soul-stirring poems, the author emphasizes the true effects of abuse: the pain, trauma, shame, and lifelong, unforgettable scars that it leaves behind on the souls of the victims. But the author also puts forward a message of hope, by reminding us that by standing together and speaking out against abuse, we can make a difference and create a safer and more compassionate world for everyone.

This book is a must-read for anyone who wants to help create a brighter future for our children and make positive changes in the world.
